Web Development Projects

Five years ago, I started to learn web development, as a way of expanding my knowledge as an AV Programmer. This not only complimented my skills within the AV industry, but also created a passion that I had not expected.

From the start, web development became an obsession, evolving into my primary professional focus. The constant learning and problem-solving challenges have kept me engaged and motivated.

Today, my greatest challenge is finding opportunities to apply my skills to real-world projects. To address this, I have started freelancing as a web developer, allowing me to collaborate with clients, expand my portfolio, and continue refining my expertise.
