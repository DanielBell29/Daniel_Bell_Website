Maths Lesson for Primary 7 Class

This project is a simple yet effective solution. With a Primary School Teacher as my partner, I offered to assist her in streamlining her lessons to enhance classroom efficiency.

After discussing the structure and flow of her lessons, I saw an opportunity to create a user-friendly website that her students could access on their tablets. This project presented an enjoyable challenge, as it allowed me to combine technical skills with educational support.

I designed a straightforward layout, incorporating the specific resources my partner requested. To maintain academic integrity, I implemented a small piece of JavaScript on the "Answers" page to ensure that students could not easily access answers without permission.

Here is the completed website. To improve accessibility, my partner created a QR code for the site and displayed it on her Smart Board, allowing students to quickly scan and navigate to the page.
