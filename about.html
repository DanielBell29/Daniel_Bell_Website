About Me

I'm Daniel Bell, an audio-visual engineer with over twelve years of experience working across various sectors, including education, heritage, and corporate environments. Throughout my career, I've developed a strong specialization in AV system design, installation, rack building, and programming. My background includes working on high-end AV installations for notable clients such as universities, local councils, oil & gas companies, and cultural heritage sites.

In addition to my expertise in AV, I've pursued web development as a natural extension of my technical skill set. What started as an interest in coding evolved into a key professional focus. I now split my time between AV engineering and web development, providing clients with comprehensive technical solutions that span both fields.

Currently, I engage in freelance web development projects alongside my AV work, taking on diverse challenges that allow me to merge these areas of expertise. Whether collaborating with clients locally or remotely, I am committed to delivering high-quality, technically sound, and user-friendly solutions.
